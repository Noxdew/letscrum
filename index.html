Features

Customisability

LetScrum has been built with customisability in mind. From colors to board stages, you can tailor it your way. More customisable features coming soon, feel free to give us suggestions.

Real-time updates

Tired of hitting the refresh button? Any change you make to a project is instantly updated for your teammates.

LetScrum Native (Coming soon)

To make sure you are always available to your team, we are going to create native applications for Windows, OS X and Linux.
